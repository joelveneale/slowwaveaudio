Project Title 2

Music Composition for Video | 2024

Project Overview

An original music composition created for a documentary film, blending orchestral elements with electronic textures to create a unique sonic identity that supports the narrative arc of the piece.

The score needed to be emotionally resonant while remaining unobtrusive, allowing the story to breathe while adding depth and atmosphere to key moments.

▶

Musical Approach

We developed a thematic approach where each major character or story thread had its own musical motif. These themes evolved throughout the film, reflecting the emotional journey and character development.

The instrumentation combined traditional string quartet recordings with modular synthesizers and processed field recordings, creating a sound that felt both timeless and contemporary.
