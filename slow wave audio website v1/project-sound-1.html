Project Title 1

Sound Design for Animation | 2024

Project Overview

This project involved creating a comprehensive soundscape for an animated short film. The brief called for an immersive audio experience that would enhance the emotional journey of the characters while maintaining a subtle, nuanced approach to the sound design.

Working closely with the director, we developed a sonic palette that complemented the visual style, using a combination of organic recordings, synthesized elements, and carefully crafted foley work.

▶

Approach

Our sound design process began with extensive field recordings to capture unique atmospheric textures. We recorded in various locations including industrial sites, natural environments, and urban spaces to build a diverse library of source material.

The final mix features over 200 individual sound elements, each carefully placed and processed to create depth and spatial awareness throughout the piece.
